1. Overview

AgriConnect connects farmers with global buyers to sell value-added farm products and promote rural entrepreneurship.

2. Functional Requirements

  • Farmers: Register, add products, manage inventory.
  • Buyers: Browse, order, and give feedback.
  • Admin: Manage users, products, and transactions.

3. Non-Functional Requirements

  • User-friendly and secure interface.
  • Fast performance and reliability.
  • Cross-browser compatibility.

4. System Requirements

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JS
  • Backend: (optional) Node.js / Flask
  • Hosting: GitHub Pages / Netlify

5. Future Enhancements

  • AI pricing, online payment, delivery tracking.
  • Mobile app and multi-language support.
